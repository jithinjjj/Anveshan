Welcome to Vidwan

Expert Database and Researcher Network

VIDWAN is the premier database of profiles of Student researchers and other faculty members working at Reva University. It provides important information about expert's background, contact address, experience, scholarly publications, skills and accomplishments, researcher identity, etc. The database developed and maintained by Reva University with financial support from the Funding organizations. The database would be instrumental in selection of panels of experts for various committees, taskforce, established by the university for monitoring and evaluation purposes.
